About Me

I am a Principal Researcher and Director of Tencent Jarvis Lab. My research interests include medical large language models, medical NLP, medical imaging, and multimodal learning.

Prior to Tencent, I worked as Senior Applied Scientist Manager at Microsoft and Staff Researcher at IBM China Research Lab. I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (advisor: Prof. Yong Yu), and my B.S. in Computer Science & Engineering from Southeast University.
